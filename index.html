about stray kids

stray kids is a self proclaimed group under the industry JYPE entertainment,the group was originally brought together through a south korean reality survival show also called stray kids that aired in 2017. although the survival show was a emotional rollercoaster the group still debuted. stray kids is well known for thier engaging music videos that undergoes meticulous editing processes and their creative lyrics stray kids produces their song by them self by a unit called 3RACHA, which consits of the following members han changbin and bangchan. the group memebres where choosen by the leader of the group bangchan although its not normally like that jyp asked the leader to do it entrusting him.

the group name was not determined by JYP Entertainment but conceptualized by the members themselves . it originally refers to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and later evvolved to represent the idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary the straykids fandom name is STAY

why did the ninth member(woojin)leave stray kids

After joining Stray Kids in 2018, Woojin's departure from the group in 2019 shocked fans. JYP Entertainment announced Woojin would leave the group before releasing the mini album:LEVANTER. The official statement read: “Woojin has left Stray Kids due to personal circumstances and has terminated his contract. Up until now, he was a Stray Kids member. We apologize for troubling many fans with this sudden news.” After leaving Stray Kids for "personal reasons",Woojin made his solo debut under the newly formed music agency 10x Entertainment. Woojin released his pre-debut digital single, “Still Dream,” on July 8, 2021. Later, he debuted with the mini-album The Moment: A Minor in August 2021. The debut album sold over 15,448 copies within a week of its release in South Korea, ranking at number 7 on the Circle Album Chart.
